FUN AND GAMES

While we have only been dating for around 6 months, we have been through a lot together! We’ve had some pretty incredible times and some pretty crazy ones too. So, in tribute, here are a couple of moments that stood out in the past year.

Talent Show

It was around the end of last year when you asked me if I’d like to emcee for a talent show you were putting on. Little did I know this would end up sparking something between us. It was during the talent show that we first started talking and growing closer! It was during the talent show that you first flirted with me and I started dropping my super subtle hints. It wasn’t long afterwards that we got together for our study session which was the first date of many to come.
